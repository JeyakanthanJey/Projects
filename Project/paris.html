Paris, France

About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its art, architecture, and cuisine. Famous landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Visitors enjoy romantic walks along the Seine River and vibrant café culture.

Top Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur
  • Seine River Cruise
